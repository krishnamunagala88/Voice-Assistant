PROCEDURES & ADVANCED TECHNOLOGIES
----------------------------------
1. Tummy Tuck (Abdominoplasty):
   - Flattens the abdomen by removing excess skin and fat, repositioning skin, and tightening abdominal muscles.
   - Mini Abdominoplasty: Small lower abdomen incision, ideal for minimal excess skin, umbilicus remains undisturbed.
   - Full Abdominoplasty: Focuses on entire midsection, removes sagging skin from upper/lower abdomen, tightens muscles, and forms a new belly button.
   - Circumferential Lipo-Abdominoplasty: 360-degree contouring for abdomen, back, and hips, combined with liposuction.
   - Cost in Delhi: Ranges between ₹2,00,000 and ₹3,00,000 depending on complexity.

2. Breast Augmentation (Augmentation Mammoplasty):
   - Enhances the size and appearance of breasts.
   - Silicone Gel Implants: Safe, non-cancerous, lasting 15-20 years. European or American implants used (implant cost ₹40,000 - ₹50,000).
   - Fat Transfer: Alternative using torso/thigh fat to enhance breasts.
   - Cost in Delhi: Ranges from ₹1,00,000 to ₹3,50,000 depending on techniques/implants.

3. Liposuction:
   - High Definition Liposuction using Vaser technology (sound energy to melt fat without damaging blood vessels or skin).

4. Facial Aesthetics:
   - Rhinoplasty (Nose reshaping).
   - Facial Fillers & Anti-Wrinkle treatments.
   - Silk Peel Technology: Most advanced hydrafacial using diamond tip probes and serums for exfoliation, extraction, and infusion.
   - Dermapen 4: Advanced microneedling for acne scars, pigmentation, texture, and skin tightening.

5. Other Procedures & Lip Surgery:
   - Lip Surgery: Lip reduction surgery and Lip fillers (to reshape and contour lips or enhance lip fullness/volume).
   - Gynecomastia Treatment
